FILMS

Spirited Away

An Academy Award-winning masterpiece and one of Studio Ghibli’s most iconic films. When a young girl enters a hidden world filled with spirits and strange creatures, she must find the strength to adapt and endure. This enchanting tale weaves magic, mystery, and heart into a visually breathtaking experience.

Howl's Moving Castle

A whimsical and moving fantasy set in a world of magic and machines. After a strange curse changes her appearance, a young woman finds refuge in a walking castle owned by a mysterious wizard. A story of transformation and courage, it explores the beauty in imperfection and the cost of war.

Kiki's Delivery Service

A timeless coming-of-age story filled with charm and warmth. A young witch sets out on her own to begin her magical training in a coastal town. As she builds her delivery business, she learns what it means to be independent, confident, and true to herself.

My Neighbor Totoro

A gentle and heartwarming celebration of childhood wonder. Two young sisters move to the countryside and discover magical creatures living in the nearby forest. With its quiet joy and iconic characters, this film has become a symbol of innocence and the magic of everyday life.

Grave of the Fireflies

A poignant and unforgettable portrayal of love and loss during wartime. Through the eyes of two young siblings trying to survive, this emotional story offers a powerful reflection on human resilience and the tragedies of conflict. A deeply moving film that stays with you long after it ends.

Princess Mononoke

An epic tale of nature, myth, and moral complexity. A young warrior becomes entangled in the struggle between industrial expansion and the spirits of the forest. Visually stunning and thematically rich, it challenges the boundaries between good and evil in a world under pressure.

Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind

A visionary film that laid the foundation for Studio Ghibli’s legacy. In a post-apocalyptic world threatened by toxic nature, a brave princess seeks harmony between mankind and the Earth. With strong ecological themes and breathtaking animation, it’s a bold tale of peace and hope.

Laputa: Castle in the Sky

An adventurous journey into a lost civilization above the clouds. Two young heroes uncover the secrets of a floating island while fleeing from sky pirates and military forces. A thrilling blend of mystery, science fiction, and timeless wonder.

The Wind Rises

A deeply personal and poetic historical drama. Inspired by real events, this film follows a gifted aircraft designer whose dreams soar against the backdrop of a changing world. It’s a tender meditation on ambition, beauty, and the price of progress

Porco Rosso

A stylish and melancholic tale set in the skies over the Adriatic Sea. A World War I pilot, cursed with the appearance of a pig, takes on air pirates while wrestling with his own past. With humor, action, and reflection, it’s a film like no other.

Whisper of the Heart

A gentle exploration of creativity and young inspiration. A curious teenager discovers a mysterious antique shop and begins to follow her own creative dreams. Set in a beautifully detailed Tokyo suburb, it’s a story about finding your voice and trusting your path.

Ponyo

A vibrant underwater fairy tale for all ages. A goldfish princess longs to live on land after forming a bond with a human boy. Overflowing with color and imagination, this magical adventure celebrates love, nature, and the wild wonder of childhood.

When Marnie Was There

A haunting and emotional story wrapped in mystery. A quiet girl is sent to the countryside, where she meets a mysterious girl who seems to exist in another time. This beautifully animated film explores memory, identity, and the ties that connect us across time.

The Tale of the Princess Kaguya

A hand-drawn masterpiece inspired by a 10th-century Japanese folktale. A bamboo cutter finds a glowing baby girl, whose beauty and spirit soon affect everyone around her. With its watercolor style and lyrical storytelling, it’s a visually poetic reflection on life, freedom, and longing.

The Secret World of Arrietty

A hidden world beneath the floorboards comes to life. Tiny people known as "Borrowers" live in secret under a country house, unseen by the human family above. When one of them is discovered, their delicate balance is at risk in this gentle, beautifully animated tale.

The Boy and the Heron

Studio Ghibli’s latest imaginative epic that blends fantasy and emotion. A young boy grieving the loss of his mother ventures into a mysterious world guided by a heron. This visually stunning film explores themes of loss, hope, and the boundary between life and the unknown through a deeply personal journey.

The Cat Returns

A whimsical and lighthearted adventure in a world ruled by cats. When a high school girl saves a mysterious cat, she’s swept away to a fantastical kingdom where feline royalty awaits. Full of charm and quirky characters, this film is a delightful escape into a magical realm.

From Up on Poppy Hill

A nostalgic coming-of-age story set in 1960s Yokohama. Students rally to save their beloved school clubhouse amid personal dreams and family secrets. It beautifully captures a moment of change, community, and young love in post-war Japan.

Only Yesterday

A thoughtful and introspective film about self-discovery. A woman on a countryside retreat reflects on her childhood and the choices that shaped her life. With gentle storytelling and rich memories, it explores the ties between past and present.

Pom Poko

A playful yet poignant tale about nature and survival. A group of shape-shifting raccoons fight to protect their forest home from expanding urbanization. Combining humor, folklore, and environmental themes, it’s a unique story about change and tradition.

Ocean Waves

A realistic and tender portrayal of youth and friendship. Set in a coastal town, this film follows the emotional ups and downs of high school life and young love. Its simple yet authentic storytelling captures the bittersweet moments of growing up.

On Your Mark

A striking short film blending music and animation. Two policemen break rules to rescue a mysterious winged girl in a surreal, futuristic cityscape. With no dialogue, it conveys themes of hope and freedom through stunning visuals and a powerful soundtrack.

My Neighbors the Yamadas

A quirky and humorous look at everyday family life. Through a series of animated vignettes, this film captures the funny, chaotic, and heartwarming moments of the Yamada family. Its unique sketch-like style sets it apart from other Ghibli films.

Tales from Earthsea

A darker, fantasy epic adapted from a celebrated novel series. A young prince and a wise mage confront growing shadows threatening their world. It explores themes of balance, power, and redemption with lush animation and sweeping landscapes.

Red Turtle: A Tale of an Island

A poetic, wordless meditation on human connection and nature. A man stranded on a deserted island encounters a mysterious red turtle that changes his fate. Through simple yet evocative imagery, the film explores solitude, love, and life’s cycles.

Earwig and the Witch

Studio Ghibli’s first fully 3D-animated feature. An orphaned girl is taken in by a mysterious witch, discovering her own magical abilities along the way. This film mixes whimsy and mischief with a fresh animation style.

the GHIBLIES episode 2

A playful, comedic short parody about Studio Ghibli itself. Through exaggerated animated sketches, it humorously imagines the daily lives of Ghibli’s staff. A quirky, lighthearted peek behind the curtain for fans of the studio.
